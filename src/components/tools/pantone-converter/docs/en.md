## Why Approximations?

Pantone colors are physical ink formulations designed to produce consistent colors in print. Converting them to digital color spaces (RGB, HEX) or other print models (CMYK) involves inherent limitations:

| Color System      | Purpose                                | Limitations                                    |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Pantone (PMS)** | Spot colors for precise print matching | Proprietary, requires physical swatch books    |
| **RGB/HEX**       | Screen display                         | Cannot represent all Pantone colors accurately |
| **CMYK**          | Process printing                       | Gamut is smaller than Pantone spot colors      |
| **HSL**           | Color manipulation                     | Mathematical conversion from RGB               |

## For Accurate Pantone Colors

If you need accurate Pantone color values for professional use:

1. **Purchase official Pantone products** - Pantone Formula Guides and Color Bridge guides provide authoritative conversions
2. **Use Pantone Connect** - Pantone's official digital platform for color management
3. **Consult with your printer** - Professional print shops can advise on color matching

## Data Source

Our Pantone color database includes approximately 20,000 colors from various Pantone color systems including Fashion, Home + Interiors (TCX), and others. The HEX values are community-sourced approximations.
